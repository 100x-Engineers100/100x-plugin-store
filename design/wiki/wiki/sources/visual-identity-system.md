## Summary
Complete brand identity spec: color system (primary + accent), typography (Canela + Neue Haas Grotesk), logo usage rules, photography style, AI imagery parameters.

## Key Ideas
- Primary palette: Ink (#141413), Chalk (#F5F3EE), Slate (#4A4A48), Fog (#CCCAC3)
- Accent: Ember (#D4622A) primary CTA, Sage (#6B8C6F) success, Sky (#5B8DB8) info
- Never use pure black (#000000) or white (#FFFFFF)
- Display: Canela Light/Regular. Heading/Body: Neue Haas Grotesk
- AI imagery params: editorial photography, warm natural light, desaturated, no neon
- 8px spacing grid; 12/8/4-column layout at desktop/tablet/mobile
