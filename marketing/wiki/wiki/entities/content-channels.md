## Overview
Three primary content channels with different norms, formats, and audience expectations.

## Key Facts / Usage
- **LinkedIn**: long-form performs, 1 post/week, insight-led not press-release
- **Email**: named sender when possible, 1 CTA per email, specific subject lines
- **Instagram**: visual-first, caption supports image, don't describe what they see

## Benchmarks (update quarterly)
| Channel | CTR target | Conversion target |
|---------|-----------|-------------------|
| Email campaigns | >2.5% | >5% of clicks |
| LinkedIn organic | >1.5% | — |
