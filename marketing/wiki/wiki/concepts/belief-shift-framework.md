## Definition
Campaigns work by changing what the audience believes. Define the From/To: "From: AI tools are for engineers. To: Marketers who use AI are setting benchmarks in their category."

## Why It Matters (for marketing)
Without a defined belief shift, campaigns measure clicks but don't know if they're moving the audience. The belief shift is the campaign's actual goal.

## How to Apply It
1. Write the current belief (what audience thinks before they see your campaign)
2. Write the desired belief (what they should think after)
3. Every asset, headline, and CTA should push toward the desired belief
4. Measure: do post-campaign surveys show the belief shifted?

## When to Apply It (trigger)
Campaign brief — required field alongside ICP.
