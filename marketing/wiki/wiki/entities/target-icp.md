## Overview
Primary buyer persona. Mid-career professional at a growing company who knows AI is important but hasn't systematically applied it to their work yet.

## Key Facts / Positioning
- Role: marketing manager, growth lead, content lead (25-35 years old)
- Company: 20-200 person startup or scale-up
- Pain: producing enough quality content, proving ROI, keeping up with AI-enabled competitors
- Channels: LinkedIn (primary), email newsletters, Twitter
- Current belief: "AI tools are useful but I don't have time to learn them properly"
