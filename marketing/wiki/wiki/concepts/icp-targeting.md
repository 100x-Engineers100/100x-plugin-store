## Definition
Ideal Customer Profile — the specific person this campaign is for. Defined by role, company size, industry, pain point, and psychographic profile (current belief, definition of winning, fears).

## Why It Matters (for marketing)
Copy written for everyone converts no one. ICP specificity is the single biggest driver of copy effectiveness.

## How to Apply It
For each campaign: name the ICP in one sentence ("Mid-level SaaS marketer at a 50-200 person company who knows AI is coming but hasn't started yet"). Then pick channels from the ICP targeting matrix in [[campaign-framework]].

## When to Apply It (trigger)
Before any copy is written. ICP definition is required in the campaign brief.
