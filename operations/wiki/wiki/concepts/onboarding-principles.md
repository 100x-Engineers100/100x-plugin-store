## Definition
5 documented failure modes from past onboardings, and the principles that prevent them: access provisioning timing, buddy assignment, manager check-in minimum, first task clarity, compliance deadline.

## Why It Matters (for operations)
Onboarding failures are invisible until the new hire is already disengaged. Principles turn past failures into prevention.

## How to Apply It
- Provision accounts 5+ business days before start (not the day before)
- Always assign a buddy — no exceptions
- Manager minimum: 3 check-ins in Week 1
- New hire needs a real task by Week 2
- Compliance training link must be in the welcome email and completed by end of Week 1

## When to Apply It (trigger)
Pre-boarding checklist review for every new hire.
