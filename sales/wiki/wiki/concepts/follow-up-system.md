## Definition
Post-call WhatsApp follow-up strategy. Never closes on call 1 — the follow-up is where conversion happens. Warm, direct, personalized, ends with a soft next step.

## Why It Matters (for sales)
Most leads don't convert on the call. The follow-up system is what separates reps who close from reps who just have good calls.

## How to Use It
- Send within 2 hours of call
- Reference something specific from the call (not generic "great talking to you")
- One clear next step (not multiple options)
- For "need to think about it" leads: follow up in 3 days max
- For family-decision leads: offer to send shareable summary

## When to Use It (trigger)
After every call, without exception.
