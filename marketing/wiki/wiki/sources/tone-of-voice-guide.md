## Summary
Defines brand voice in 4 pillars (honest/sharp/warm/direct), writing rules, banned phrases, channel adaptations, and tone calibration by situation.

## Key Ideas
- 4 pillars: Honest (say what we mean), Sharp (respect reader's time), Warm (notice the human), Direct (lead with the point)
- Active voice always. Contractions always. Specifics over generalities.
- Banned: "exciting announcement", "thrilled to share", "game-changing", "leverage", "synergy", "circle back"
- Max 1 exclamation mark per piece of content
- Channel differences: LinkedIn = long-form insight; Twitter = one sharp take; email = named sender
