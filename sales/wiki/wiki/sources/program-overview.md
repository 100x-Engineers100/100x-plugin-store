## Summary
Full overview of the 6-month Applied AI cohort: 3 modules (diffusion, LLMs, agents), format, pricing, success stories by domain, FAQ.

## Key Ideas
- 3 modules: Diffusion (weeks 1-6), Full-Stack LLM (7-12), AI Agents (13-24)
- 8-10 hours/week — designed for working professionals
- No coding required for Module 1; Modules 2-3 taught from scratch for non-engineers
- Pricing: ₹80,000 full / ₹14,500×6 EMI
- Success stories: engineer, designer, marketer, doctor, non-tech founder
