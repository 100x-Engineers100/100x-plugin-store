## Definition
Mapping the cohort's value proposition to the lead's specific domain and role. Not "this cohort teaches AI" but "a marketer who does this cohort runs campaigns that would've needed a 5-person team."

## Why It Matters (for sales)
Generic pitches get generic objections. Domain-specific pitches create instant credibility and make the lead feel understood.

## How to Use It
1. Identify domain from discovery (designer, doctor, founder, marketer, engineer)
2. Pull the domain-specific angle from the program overview
3. Map modules to their actual workflow: "Module 1 + your design eye = X"
4. Use a matched success story from that domain

## When to Use It (trigger)
As soon as the lead's domain is confirmed. Usually within the first 2 minutes.

## Examples from Calls
- Designer: "Your aesthetic judgment + AI tools = output a junior dev team needs 3 weeks for, in 3 days."
- Doctor: "The first wave of AI health tools are built by tech people who don't understand medicine. You do."
