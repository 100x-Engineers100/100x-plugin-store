# Marketing Wiki — Overview

## Purpose
Second brain for the marketing team. Brand voice, campaign intelligence, competitive context — so every output sounds like us and learns from what worked.

## Three Moments
- **Campaign planning:** Goal + audience → campaign brief with ICP context + past learnings
- **Content creation:** Format + topic + channel → on-brand copy with correct voice and hooks
- **Competitive response:** Competitor move → intel + positioning framing + counter-messaging

## Brand Voice State
Four pillars: **Honest. Sharp. Warm. Direct.**
No exclamation spam, no banned phrases (see [[tone-of-voice-guide]]), active voice always.
