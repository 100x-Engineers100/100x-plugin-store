## Definition
Structured brief format that anchors every design project to brand context before any creative work begins: project type, audience, brand context, precedents, direction.

## Why It Matters (for design)
Briefs without brand context produce work that's good in isolation but off-system. The framework ensures brand intelligence is loaded before creative decisions start.

## How to Apply It
Required fields: project type, target audience, key message (1 sentence), brand guidelines ref, relevant precedents (2-3), what to avoid, deliverables + sizes, deadline.

## When to Apply It (trigger)
Before starting any design project. No exceptions — even quick one-offs need at minimum: audience, key message, brand ref.
