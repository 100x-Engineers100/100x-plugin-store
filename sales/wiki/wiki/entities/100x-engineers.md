## Overview
100xEngineers runs a 6-month Applied AI cohort for working professionals. 3 modules: Diffusion (weeks 1-6), Full-Stack LLM (7-12), AI Agents (13-24).

## Key Facts / Role
- Price: ₹80,000 full / ₹14,500×6 EMI
- Time commitment: 8-10 hours/week
- Format: live sessions (recorded) + async project work
- Cohort size: ~100-150 per cohort
- No coding required for Module 1
