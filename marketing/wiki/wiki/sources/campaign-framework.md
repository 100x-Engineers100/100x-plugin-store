## Summary
Full campaign planning framework: 3-question brief test, AIDA stages, ICP targeting matrix, brief template, messaging architecture (L1/L2/L3), metrics reference, post-mortem template.

## Key Ideas
- 3 pre-copy questions: who exactly, what belief shift, what single CTA
- AIDA: awareness→interest→desire→action — define which stages the campaign targets
- Messaging L1 (10-word claim) → L2 (3 supporting points) → L3 (evidence). Write L3→L1, approve L1→L3
- One CTA per campaign — never two
- Post-mortem within 1 week of close: what worked, what didn't, assets to reuse
