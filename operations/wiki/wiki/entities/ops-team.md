## Overview
Core operations team with defined ownership areas for SOPs, incidents, HR, and IT.

## Key Facts / Responsibilities
- **Operations Manager**: SOP owner, account provisioning, incident comms lead, process documentation
- **HR Lead**: employment docs, payroll, benefits, HRIS, compliance training
- **Engineering Lead**: incident commander (P0/P1), on-call rotation owner, post-mortem lead
- **IT/DevOps**: device setup, system access, security training, PagerDuty config
- **Hiring Manager**: role-specific onboarding, 30-day check-in, buddy assignment
