## Summary
Pre-boarding to 30-day checklist for all full-time employees. 4 roles: Ops Manager, HR Lead, Hiring Manager, Buddy. Escalation paths and 5 documented common failure points.

## Key Steps / Process
- Pre-boarding (1 week before): account provisioning, device, welcome email, buddy assignment
- Day 1: welcome 1:1, compliance training, tool setup, buddy intro
- Week 1: intro calls, benefits enrollment, first check-in Day 3
- Weeks 2-4: first real task, 30-day check-in with structured template

## Owners / Responsibilities
- Account provisioning: Operations Manager
- Employment docs/payroll: HR Lead
- Role onboarding + 30-day check-in: Hiring Manager
- Culture guide/day-to-day: Buddy
