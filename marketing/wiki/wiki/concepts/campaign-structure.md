## Definition
AIDA framework applied to campaigns: Awareness → Interest → Desire → Action. Each stage has distinct goals, formats, and success metrics. Campaigns should target specific stages, not all four.

## Why It Matters (for marketing)
Campaigns that try to do everything (awareness + conversion) do neither well. Stage clarity forces the right format, message, and CTA.

## How to Apply It
Define which stage(s) this campaign targets before briefing. Match formats to stage:
- Awareness: social, PR, SEO content
- Interest: long-form, case studies, comparison
- Desire: demos, trials, testimonials
- Action: sales conversations, limited offers

## When to Apply It (trigger)
Campaign brief creation — first decision after defining goal.
