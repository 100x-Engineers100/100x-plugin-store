## Definition
3-mode system for responding to any objection based on the lead's energy: Warm (curious), Pressure (resistant), Direct (analytical).

## Why It Matters (for sales)
Same objection, wrong mode = pushback. Reading the lead's energy and selecting the right mode converts resistance into momentum.

## How to Use It
| Signal | Mode | Approach |
|--------|------|----------|
| Still asking questions | Warm | Open-ended, explore their reality |
| Repeating the objection | Pressure | Reframe with analogy (IT wave → AI wave) |
| Wants specifics/numbers | Direct | Give data, ROI, concrete comparison |
| "I'll think about it" twice | Uncover | Find the real objection first |

## When to Use It (trigger)
Any time a lead raises an objection.
