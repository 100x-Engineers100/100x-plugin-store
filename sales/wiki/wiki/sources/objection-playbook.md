## Summary
Covers 5 core objections with 3 response modes each (warm/pressure/direct). Includes meta-framework for selecting the right mode based on lead signals.

## Key Ideas
- Every objection is a disguised question: "Why is this worth it for me specifically?"
- Mode selection based on lead energy: curious→warm, resistant→pressure, analytical→direct
- Never discount on first objection — signals price was never real
- "Not the right time" = prioritization signal, not a calendar problem
- Family/partner objections are legitimate — treat differently from stalling

## Notable Quotes
> "My goal is not to convince them. My goal is to make them confront their reality."
