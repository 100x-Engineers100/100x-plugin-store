# 100xEngineers Applied AI Cohort — Program Overview

## What It Is
A 6-month, cohort-based applied AI program for working professionals. Not a course. Not a bootcamp. A structured, project-driven learning system that takes anyone — engineer, designer, marketer, founder, doctor — from AI-curious to AI-capable.

---

## The Three Modules

### Module 1: Diffusion & Generative Visuals (Weeks 1-6)
Learn to generate, control, and productionize AI imagery and video.

**Tools covered:** Stable Diffusion, ComfyUI, Flux, ControlNet, LoRA training, HeyGen, ElevenLabs

**What you'll build:**
- AI influencer pipeline
- Product photography system
- Brand asset generator
- Short-form video production workflow

**Who gets the most from this:** Designers, marketers, content creators, founders, animators

---

### Module 2: Full-Stack LLM Applications (Weeks 7-12)
Learn to build, deploy, and evaluate LLM-powered products.

**Tools covered:** OpenAI/Anthropic APIs, LangChain, FastAPI, RAG, structured outputs, evaluation frameworks

**What you'll build:**
- Customer-facing chatbot with retrieval
- LLM evaluation harness
- Domain-specific document Q&A system
- Full-stack AI web app

**Who gets the most from this:** Engineers, product managers, founders, analysts

---

### Module 3: AI Agents (Weeks 13-24)
Learn to build multi-step autonomous agents that execute real workflows.

**Tools covered:** LangGraph, CrewAI, tool calling, Durable Objects, MCP servers, production deployment

**What you'll build:**
- Multi-agent workflow automation
- AI that can browse, write, and execute
- Production-grade agentic system
- Personal second brain / knowledge system

**Who gets the most from this:** Engineers, founders, operators, anyone building AI products

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: Do I need to know how to code?**
No. Module 1 (diffusion) requires no coding. Modules 2 and 3 involve code but are taught from scratch for non-engineers. Designers, marketers, and doctors have completed all 3 modules.

**Q: Can I do this while working full-time?**
Yes. Designed for working professionals. 8-10 hours/week is the commitment. Live sessions are recorded.

**Q: What makes this different from Coursera or YouTube?**
Live cohort, real projects, peer accountability, and curriculum built by people actively building AI products in production — not academics.

**Q: Is there a refund policy?**
Yes. First 2 weeks, full refund if not satisfied. After that, pro-rated based on modules completed.
