## Definition
Zahid's structure for sales calls: discovery first, pitch second, never close on call 1. Discovery uncovers the lead's reality; pitch maps the cohort to that reality.

## Why It Matters (for sales)
Reps who pitch too early get objections. Reps who discover first get the lead to articulate their own pain — which is far more persuasive than anything the rep says.

## How to Use It
1. Open with context-setting (who you are, 60 sec)
2. Ask 2-3 discovery questions (domain, current role, AI exposure)
3. Listen — don't fill silence
4. Map cohort modules to what they said
5. Handle objections with wiki intelligence
6. Never close on call 1 — set up the follow-up

## When to Use It (trigger)
Every inbound or outbound call. No exceptions.

## Examples from Calls
"What are you working on right now that AI could help with?" → leads to Module 2 or 3 mapping.
